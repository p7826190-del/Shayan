Executive Summary               Quran Mubarak is a long-established (15 years) online Quran academy with a simple but functional lead-generation website. Its core value proposition is affordable ($40–50/month), one-on-one live Quran tutoring with a free 3-class trial to reduce enrollment friction.                        However, the platform is fundamentally a marketing/inquiry website with no digital product backbone. It lacks any LMS, student portal, online payment, or modern learning tools. The site has also been compromised and contains injected spam links, which is a serious credibility and SEO liability.                        The competitive opportunity is clear: match their low-friction free trial and affordable pricing, then dramatically out-execute on trust signals (teacher profiles, testimonials), product quality (LMS, progress tracking), and user experience (modern design, mobile app, instant payment). Quran Mubarak wins on price and brand age — you can win on trust, technology, and transparency.
